Experience the Difference

Sparky's Coffee House provides the experience of consuming a high-quality specialty coffee. More than a beverage, our delicious coffee begins with 100% brutte arabic coffee beans that are grown in India at an altitude of 1500m above sea level which helps to produce a more desired coffee flavor. Come experience the difference at Sparky's Coffee House.

  • Small batch roasting to ensure unparalleled quality and freshness
  • Flavored coffees for sale
  • Live acoustic music on Thursday and Saturday nights
  • Showcase your artwork to sell in our cafe
